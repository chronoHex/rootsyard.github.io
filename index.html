The yard

Situated in the heart of the Peki community, surrounded by rolling hills running parallel to Lake Volta, Roots Yard is an Eco-Friendy Resort perfect for relaxing and experiencing traditional and contemporary Ghanaian life

Location

We are located in Peki-Avetile, just after GOVCO junction, on the Akosombo/Hohoe Road, in the Volta Region, allowing for easy exploration of the various attractions of the area, such as the Tafi Atome Monkey Sanctuary, Afadzato (the Highest Mnt in Ghana) & Wli Falls (the Tallest waterfall in West Africa)

Our Vision

Our aim is to offer, both visitors and locals alike, a comfortable & homely, relaxing place to enjoy some good, healthy food and drinks in a mellow atmosphere of communication and respect.
